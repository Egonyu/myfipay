How it works

From sign-up to your first paying customer in three steps.

1

Sign up and set your prices

Create your account, add your hotspot location, and set your own plans — 1 hour, 1 day, 1 week, any price in UGX you choose.

2

Connect your router

Point your MikroTik hotspot at myFiPay. Anyone who joins your WiFi sees your branded portal with your name, colours, and plans.

3

Customers pay, WiFi unlocks

They enter their phone number, approve the mobile-money prompt, and they're online in seconds. You watch every shilling arrive in your dashboard.
